Specifications.
Project Purpose
To create a searchable database where users can search for warnings of triggering content such as sexual abuse, torture, cheating (relationships), animal death, and strong language that may or may not be obvious upon engaging with media.Therefore, preventing those who are survivors of abuse, who are diagnosed with PTSD and other mental illnessess or just those who prefer not to see specific content would be able to curate their media content, without having to go through negative experiences (and triggering episodes, if applicable).

Although marketed as a website for users that want to watch certain media with family -- namely their parents -- it is within our goal as well to provide services for those that need a service such as this to get a better understanding of what type of media they will be interacted with.

The aim is not to spoil the movie, or to ‘ruin’ plot twists, but we here at Warning Watchers, want to encourage a better, more self-aware, more prepared culture of media engagement in the world. We acknowledge that everyone is unique and have their own likes, dislikes, and boundaries as individuals and we hope to provide a tool that will help prevent negative experiences with content that could’ve been easily avoided.
