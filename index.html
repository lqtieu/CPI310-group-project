Warning Watchers
"Can you watch that with your parents?"
Uncomfortable with spiders? Afraid of random jump scares? Prefer not to see unnecessarily long scenes of public displays of affection? Warning Watchers compiles a searchable database of media that you can look at ahead of time to see if a movie is suitable for not only your family to watch together, but for yourself, friends, and anyone else you want to get together with!
